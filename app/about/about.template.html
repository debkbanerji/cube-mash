What is CubeMash?

CubeMash is a web app that allows you to compare twisty puzzles. Playing is fairly straightforward; you're presented with two puzzles and can either choose which one you prefer or skip to the next comparison. You can then view stats to see where puzzles rank in terms of popularity.

So you're trying to see which puzzles are better than others...

The quality of a puzzle can be quite subjective. I don't believe in the concept of a single 'best' puzzle. CubeMash is simply a way to see which puzzles are more popular than others. You may not always like an extremely popular puzzle, because liking a puzzle often comes down to personal preference.

Where did you get the data from?

I built the database using data from TheCubicle.us.

The database was last updated on 3rd July, 2016.

How did you choose the puzzle categories?

There is one category for each official WCA puzzle, except for clock.

Why no clock?

Because there is basically only one good clock on the market.
